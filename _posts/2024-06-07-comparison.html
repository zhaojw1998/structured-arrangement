Ours vs Baselines

We compare our system with three existing methods for accompaniment arrangement: 1) PopMAG, 2) Anticipatory Music Transformer (AMT), and 3) GETMusic. The first two are Transformer-based and the last one is a diffusion model. The arrangement results are presented with five tabs in the following order: 1) Ours, 2) AMT, 3) GETMusic, 3) PopMAG, and 3) the input lead sheet.
